Frequently Asked Questions

Whether you're new to our platform or a seasoned user, we hope that these FAQs will provide you with the information you need to get the most out of our services. If you can't find the answer you're looking for, please don't hesitate to contact us.

  • How do I create a business profile on your platform?

    Creating a business profile on our platform is easy! Simply sign up for an account, fill in your business information, and start creating posts and ads to promote your offerings.

  • What types of businesses can use your platform?

    Our platform is designed for businesses of all types and sizes, from small startups to large enterprises. Whether you're a retailer, service provider, or anything in between, we can help you reach your target audience and grow your brand.

  • How do reviews and ratings work on your platform?

    Our review and rating system is designed to promote transparency and honesty. All reviews are written by real customers who have interacted with businesses on our platform. Customers can rate businesses based on their experiences and provide detailed feedback to help other potential customers make informed decisions.

  • How do I get started with your platform?

    Getting started with our platform is easy! Simply sign up for an account, create your business profile, and start creating posts, ads, and catalogs to promote your offerings. If you have any questions or need help getting started, our support team is always here to help.

  • How does your targeted advertising feature work?

    Our targeted advertising feature allows businesses to create ads that reach specific demographics, interests, or other criteria. You can choose the audience you want to target and create ads that are tailored to their needs and interests, helping you increase your ROI and get the most out of your advertising budget.
